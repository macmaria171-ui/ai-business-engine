# AI Business Engine

Automated AI-powered business generating digital products and services 24/7.

## System Architecture

- **Product Factory**: Generates digital products (prompt packs, templates, ebooks, code)
- **Gumroad Publisher**: Lists products on Gumroad for sale
- **Fiverr Manager**: Creates and optimizes Fiverr service gigs
- **Marketing Engine**: Auto-posts to social media and Telegram
- **Analytics**: Revenue tracking and reporting

## Workflows

- `product-factory.yml` - Generates new products every 48h
- `gumroad-publish.yml` - Publishes products to Gumroad daily
- `fiverr-gigs.yml` - Optimizes Fiverr gigs daily
- `social-marketing.yml` - Posts marketing content every 6h
- `analytics-report.yml` - Daily revenue report
